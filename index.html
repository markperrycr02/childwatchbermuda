About Us

ChildWatch is an all volunteer non-profit self-funded action group of concerned parents, initially fathers, serving the citizens of Bermuda and Bermudian overseas. Our aim is to effect change in the laws, and society's views on the rights of single and divorced fathers, to actively share in the day-to-day rearing of their children and, to even be the primary cargiver.
