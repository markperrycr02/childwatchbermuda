About Us
Who we are
ChildWatch is an action group of concerned parents, initially fathers. Our aim is to effect change in the laws and society's views on the rights of single and divorced fathers, to actively share in the day-to-day rearing of their children and, to even be the primary cargiver.
Why we formed
To address issues pertaining to the unjust treatment of responsible and caring Fathers in relationship to theor parental rights over the welfare and upbringing of their children and, to bring focus and improvement on the enforcement of the custody laws.
Mission Statement
