Our Objectives
Keep the well being of the child the focus of parenting for both parties
Champion the child’s rights to enjoy equal access to both biological parents.
Promote fair an unbiased judgements in child custody cases.
Ensure that fathers are given equal opportunity to provide a positive male role in their child’s day-to-day life.
Educate society on the vital role and significance of the father in the child’s life.
Mentor and lend support to disenfranchised fathers who are trying to a positive role in their child’s life.
Provide mediation for couples and in particular fathers, so that children are as unaffected as possible during a separation or divorce.
